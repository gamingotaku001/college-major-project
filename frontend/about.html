OUR STORY

Building Hope Through Education

Founded with the vision of creating better educational opportunities for the people of the town, this institution was started by Md Ethesham Sohrab .

For years, students had very limited access to affordable and high-quality education. Many schools lacked proper guidance, personal attention, and support for students.

The goal was simple but powerful: create a school where every student could learn, grow, and receive proper guidance regardless of financial background.

What started as a dream shared by him and his wife slowly grew into a mission supported by many people working together toward a better future for education.

“Education is not only about creating toppers — it is about creating capable human beings.”
